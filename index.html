About Us

About Us

Headway Media & Entertainment is a media company headquartered in the majestic Victoria falls. It operates around Southern Africa, in Zambia, Zimbabwe and Botswana. Headway specializes in providing visual and audio services for various occasions, indoors and outdoors. Headway is also involved in the entertainment industry as it also organizes and hosts live performance shows. With a tightly knit group of talented and experienced technicians in audio and visual equipment, the company has strived for excellence, as it has successfully hosted events for Local and international clientele.
